Constraints

Quiet rules that guide every part of our design— gentle, intentional, and always human-centered.

01. Soft Contrast Only

We avoid harsh color clashes and heavy shadows. Every palette is tuned to stay warm, breathable, and kind to the eyes.

02. Minimal Motion

Animations are subtle and purposeful. Nothing moves just for show, and nothing distracts from the content.

03. Focus on Essentials

We keep layouts simple, remove unnecessary noise, and let just a few strong elements tell the whole story.
